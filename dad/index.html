General Dermatology

Dermatology is the field of medicine that is concerned with diseases of the skin, hair and nails. The skin is the largest organ of the body. It serves as both a multi-functional organ and an interface with the environment. There are more diseases of the skin than of any other organ. Healthy skin is essential to good health.

The training of a dermatologist includes four years of medical school, one year of internship and three years of intensive dermatology residency.
