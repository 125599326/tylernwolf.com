Description

The Garden for Cardboard is an educational app in which users explore the details of high-resolution 3D scans of real works of art. Users enter the Garden and can explore the space around them. They can then enter an inspection mode for a given sculpture and take a closer look a the finer details of the artwork.

The Garden for Cardboard is currently available on Android here. An iOS version is under development and will be released shortly. The website can be found here.
