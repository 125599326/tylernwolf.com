Alpha vs. Correlation/Dispersion

The top three charts on the left display Alpha over time for a Fund, Hedge Fund Index A (IDX A) and Hedge Fund Index B (IDX B), respectively. At the bottom is the correlation and dispersion plotted over the same time period. On the right, a scatter plot shows the average Alpha for the three candidates in given market conditions (high/low correlation in combination with high/low dispersion). In the bottom right are the details of the currently focused data point.

To focus on a data point, either in the bar charts or the scatter plot, mouse over the data point - the other charts will highlight the corresponding point and the detail section will update with the corresponding numbers. See it on github
